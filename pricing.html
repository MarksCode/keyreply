Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free 30-day trial work?

After you create an account, you have instant access to all of KeyReply's features for 30 days. During this period you can upgrade at any time by visiting the change plan page.

You don’t need a credit card during the trial, so feel free to try it out.

What happens after the free 30-day trial?

Towards the end of the 30 days we will email you and check if you want to upgrade. If you feel ready to buy a paid plan you can do so using a credit card or PayPal. You can also enter your payment details anytime during the trial period to avoid any disruption.

If you choose not to upgrade during the trial period, your plan will be automatically discontinued. If you choose to upgrade later, your team will have access to all your team's KeyReplies again.

What does my team have to do?

Your team members should download the KeyReply mobile app, enable the keyboard and log in using the team credentials you have created.

Changes to the templates and categories are synchronized for the entire team.

If you have any questions at all, please do reach out to us via support@keyreply.com or send us a text on our contact number.
