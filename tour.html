Let's take a tour of KeyReply

Find out more about the features & details that could really help your team using KeyReply.

The Keyboard

The KeyReply keyboard is where you get to quickly use your replies.

The keyboard is an input method - which basically means that the keyboard follows you wherever you are typing. You basically get access to your KeyReplies everywhere across your phone.

So for example, if you team needs to do customer service or operations over chat, email, text, calls, and so on - they can still access these replies on all these channels.

The results are increased efficiency while working on mobile and also lots of saved time from repetition. In a study we ran, potential time saving per person comes up to 70 minutes a day (a whole lunch time) and 12 days a year (vacation time). Imagine what you, and your team, could have done with that time!

The KeyReplies

KeyReplies are the content that you use on your keyboard.

You can manage these KeyReplies on both the app and web, which means easily adding and sharing these KeyReplies across the team.

By enabling the knowledgebase of responses everywhere on team members' phones, you can ensure consistency and standards across replies, and across people. This can be extremely important in sales and service, since stakeholders' satisfaction is highly reliant on their interactions.

You can also add new KeyReplies from your keyboard while you type new responses, and then go back and share these new responses with those you think may need it.

It's also a perfect way to onboard new team members, by putting the information in their hands right from the start.

The structure, context & services


When you enable structured communications among the team, you will find that there is less ambiguity in their jobs and dealings with stakeholders. This role clarity is imperative to increased employee happiness.

Not everyone that talks to you will have a good idea how to explain their issues, so by putting in place standard questions can help to set the right context for the conversation.

We believe in the power of setting up structured forms of communications - and that's why we're offering to help you get started, improve, audit and migrate some of these practices on to KeyReply for everyone's satisfaction.
