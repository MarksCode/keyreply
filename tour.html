How KeyReply works



It's really easy to get started on KeyReply.


  • Add replies: Create or import your templates on the app or web.
  • Start replying: Open any chat, email, or typing app as you normally would.
  • Input: Switch keyboard input to "KeyReply" by holding down the globe button.
  • Choose: Pick from your categories; click through to select a template.
  • Edit: Edit your reply if needed.
  • Done: Send! Time saved, effort saved, and messages are structured as you like them.
