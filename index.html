Why efficient communications is a problem today

70% of replies are repetitive

Almost 70% of all replies that sales, operation, support or administrative staff send in a day are repetitive. That means it's been addressed before, but not properly catalogued.

Repetition = Waste:

  • Time wasted on finding, copying, and sending these replies again.
  • Efficiency is compromised.
  • Employee satisfaction goes down (as boredom increases).

Structure & context varies

Consistency across people can be hard to control, even for the same issues. Plus, not every person asking or answering questions has clarity as to how they should phrase things.

Ambiguity = Mess:

  • Back and forth just to get to the root cause.
  • Frustration at the progress made in discussions.
  • Potential strains in relationships over time.

Replying on mobile is tough

If you've typed on a mobile, you know. But since these days work is mobile, and 24/7, around the world, there can be complications in enabling new working styles.

Hassle = Inefficient:

  • Not having everyone on the same page with the right resources.
  • Delayed response until you get to the laptop or desk.
  • Reduced capabilities and knowledgebase on the go.
